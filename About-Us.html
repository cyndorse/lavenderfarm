Purple Haze Lavender Farm

About Us

Purple Haze Lavender Farm located in Labadie, Missouri is nestled among the rolling hills of Missouri South of all the Missouri River adjacent to Missouri Wine Country. With many local bed and breakfast businesses in the area and the Katy trail so close, a visit to our farm makes a nice addition to your weekend plan. Come enjoy the aroma of our lavender and sit along the crops of beautiful fragrant lavender with a bottle of wine, or enjoy and adventurous gardening experience and cut your very own bundle.
