Munstead English Lavender

Munstead English Lavender, also known as English lavender, a beautiful compact shrub. It has gray foliage, and an abundance of fragrant, lavender-colored flower spikes. Missouri Botanical Garden state that Munstead English lavender is ideal for using in perfumes, sachets and oils.

Vera English Lavender

Vera English lavender is often regarded as one of the original types of lavenders. It is one of the best oil producing lavenders, so it ideal for aromatherapy and makes a nice gift. English lavender is also used in cooking, herbal teas and as a sleep aid.
